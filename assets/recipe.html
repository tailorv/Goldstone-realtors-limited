Fried Eggs, Broccoli & Orange Juice recipe



10 mins

Prep Time



25 mins

Cook Time



Serves

2-4 People

Preparation steps

  • Beat two eggs in a bowl
  • Chop two large Onions
  • Cut 3 large tomatoes
  • Pre-heat oil in a pan
  • pour in onions
  • Add paprika, masala and soy sauce
  • Pour in the beaten eggs
  • Add whole brocolli
  • Beat two eggs in a bowl
  • Mix till Golden Brown
  • Serve while hot
  • Serve with Orange Juice for maximum flavour

Ingredients:

  1. Two eggs
  2. Three tomatoes
  3. Cut 3 large tomatoes
  4. Brocolli
  5. Two Onions
  6. 1 cup cooking oil
  7. Masala
  8. I cup Orange Juice
